My journey to become a web developer..

On the 17th of May 2023, I decided to take a step towards becoming a web developer. Before then, I did the typical YouTube tutorials and other free online resources which really helped me build this new passion, especially by watching other people go through what sounds like the road I have just embarked on!

My enrollment into "The Complete 2023 Web Development Bootcamp" by Dr. Angela Yu was my way of putting (some of) my money where my mouth is!

I've linked to some of the projects dotted along my journey thus far so have a look!
